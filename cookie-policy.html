Cookie Usage Policy

1. Introduction

This Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on our educational platform website. Our goal is to provide a safe, accessible, and user-friendly learning experience for all users.

2. What are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help enhance the functionality of the platform, remember your preferences, and provide personalized educational content.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the operation of the educational platform and allow users to access core features, such as navigating courses, accessing learning materials, and saving progress.

3.2 Functional Cookies

Functional cookies remember your preferences, such as language selection, course settings, and other customizations, to make your educational experience smoother and more personalized.

3.3 Analytical Cookies

We use these cookies to analyze user engagement, understand how learners interact with our platform, and improve the delivery of expert-led educational content and resources.

4. How We Use Cookies

  • Ensure proper functionality of the educational platform
  • Remember user preferences and learning progress
  • Analyze platform usage to improve the educational experience
  • Provide personalized academic content and recommendations

5. Managing Cookies

You can configure your browser to refuse cookies or delete existing cookies. Please note that this may affect some functionalities of the platform, including access to saved learning progress and personalized recommendations.

6. Links to Other Websites

Our platform may include links to third-party educational websites. We do not control cookies on those sites and recommend reviewing their own cookie policies.

7. Changes to the Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. All changes will be published on this page, and users are encouraged to review updates regularly.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding cookie usage on our educational platform, please contact us using the contact details provided on the website.
